Invoice QR Studio Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Invoice QR Studio helps you create invoices and QR codes and get paid faster. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Use

Invoice QR Studio stores the content you create — your business profile, clients, items, tax rates, invoices, payment records, brand kit (logo, colors, fonts), and the QR codes you design — together with your app preferences and your Pro purchase entitlement status.

This content is created by you and kept on your device. The app does not require an account and does not ask you to sign in.

How Information Is Used

We use this information only to provide the app's features: creating and managing invoices, tracking payment status, designing QR codes, applying your brand, and unlocking Pro features you purchase.

Invoice QR Studio does not sell your personal information, and does not use your data to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Storage And Sharing

Your invoices, clients, and designs are stored on your device. If you enable an Apple system service such as iCloud backup, that content is stored or synced according to your Apple settings and Apple's terms.

When you share or export an invoice or QR code, it is sent only where you direct it using the standard iOS share sheet — for example to Messages, Mail, Files, or another app you choose.

A payment QR code encodes the payment link or invoice details that you provide. The app itself does not process payments and does not receive your customers' payment card details.

Purchases

Invoice QR Studio Pro is sold through Apple In-App Purchase. Apple processes the transaction; the app receives only your product and entitlement status, not your full payment card details. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple Account settings.

Permissions

If you grant photo access to add a logo to your brand kit or an invoice, that permission is used only for the feature you requested. You can change app permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Some features may stop working if a required permission is disabled.

Data Control

You can edit or delete your invoices, clients, items, and QR designs inside the app at any time. Removing the app removes its locally stored data from your device. For privacy questions, use the support channel listed on the App Store product page.

Children

Invoice QR Studio is a business productivity app and is not directed to children, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The latest version will be available from the public Privacy Policy URL listed in App Store Connect.
