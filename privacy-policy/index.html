BeforeGo Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

BeforeGo helps you plan trips, manage checklists, and save travel memories. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Use

BeforeGo may store trip details, destinations, dates, checklist items, notes, photos, voice memories, place information you choose to add, app preferences, purchase entitlement status, and basic app diagnostics or analytics.

If you grant microphone, camera, photo, or location access, BeforeGo uses that permission only for the feature you requested, such as recording a voice memory, adding a photo, or attaching a place to a memory.

How Information Is Used

We use information to provide trip planning, checklist, widget, Live Activity, memory, purchase, support, analytics, and app improvement features.

BeforeGo does not sell your personal information.

Storage And Sharing

Your trip content is stored on your device unless a system service you enable, such as iCloud backup or widgets, stores or displays it according to Apple settings.

BeforeGo may use Apple services for purchases and device features. VIP purchases are processed by Apple In-App Purchase. BeforeGo receives product and entitlement status, not your full payment card details.

BeforeGo may use Firebase or Apple services for analytics, crash diagnostics, product improvement, and purchase-related operation. These providers process data under their own terms and privacy commitments.

Permissions

You can change app permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Some features may stop working if a required permission is disabled.

Data Control

You can delete trips, notes, memories, and checklist content inside the app. You can also contact the app owner through the support channel listed on the App Store product page for privacy support.

Children

BeforeGo is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The latest version should be available from the app and from the public Privacy Policy URL listed in App Store Connect.

Contact

Use the support channel listed on the App Store product page for privacy support.
